What is the duration of each coaching session?

Each one-on-one coaching session typically lasts for 60 minutes. However, this can be adjusted based on your specific needs and goals.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. The new plan will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How do I schedule my coaching sessions?

Once you sign up for a plan, you'll receive an email with a link to our scheduling platform. You can choose the time slots that work best for you.

What kind of support do you offer?

Depending on your plan, you’ll have access to email, chat, or phone support. We also offer a priority support option for Pro, Premium, and VIP plan members.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session?

We understand that life happens! You can cancel or reschedule your session up to 24 hours in advance without any penalties.

Are the coaching sessions conducted online or in person?

All coaching sessions are conducted online via Zoom, Skype, or another video conferencing tool. This allows you to connect from anywhere in the world.
